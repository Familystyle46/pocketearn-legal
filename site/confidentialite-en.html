1. Data controller

The controller responsible for personal data collected via the Tiipee app is:

  • Name: Tiipee (sole proprietorship)
  • Email: contact@tiipee.com
  • Country: France

2. Data collected

2.1 Parent account

  • Email address (login identifier)
  • First name
  • Pocket-money rule configuration (amounts, time ranges)
  • History of validated payouts
  • Subscription status

2.2 Child account

  • First name only (no email, no date of birth)
  • Duration of "phone put down" sessions (timestamped data)
  • Total daily screen time (app usage duration, via the Android "usage access" permission) — only the aggregate duration, never per-app detail or content. This feature is optional and only enabled if the child allows it.
  • Accumulated earnings (calculated automatically)
  • Family invite code (temporary)
Important: We never collect the content of apps used, browsing history, location, contacts, or any other personal data of the child beyond what is listed above.

2.3 Technical data

  • Anonymous device identifier (for notifications)
  • Anonymized error logs
  • App and OS version
  • Aggregated usage statistics (screens viewed, usage events, device type, approximate country/region) collected via Firebase Analytics using a technical app instance identifier. This data is only used for audience measurement and service improvement; it is never used for advertising purposes and no advertising identifier (IDFA) is used.

3. Protection of minors

Tiipee is designed for children aged 13 to 17. In accordance with GDPR (Article 8) and French law, a child's account can only be created by a parent or legal guardian.

3.1 Mandatory parental consent

Creating a child profile requires:

  • A pre-existing, verified parent account
  • An invite code sent by the parent
  • The parent's explicit acceptance of these terms

3.2 Minimized data

The child profile only contains their first name. No email address, date of birth, or identity data is collected directly from the child.

3.3 Parental access

The parent has access to all of their child's data on Tiipee and can request its deletion at any time from the app or by contacting us.

5. Retention periods

  • Active account: data retained for the entire duration of use
  • After account deletion: permanent deletion within 30 days
  • Sessions and earnings: retained for 24 months (history needed by parents)
  • Billing data: 10 years (legal accounting obligation)
  • Technical logs: 90 days

7. Hosting and security

  • Location: Servers hosted in France (AWS Paris / eu-west-3)
  • Encryption: All communications are encrypted via TLS 1.3
  • Database: Encryption at rest (AES-256)
  • Authentication: Hashed passwords (bcrypt), never stored in plain text
  • Access: PostgreSQL Row Level Security — each family only accesses its own data
  • Backups: Daily encrypted backups, 7-day retention

8. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: delete your account and all your data
  • Right to data portability: receive your data in a readable format
  • Right to object: object to certain processing
  • Right to restriction: suspend processing without deleting the data
  • Rights regarding minors: the parent can exercise all of these rights on behalf of their child

To exercise your rights, contact us at contact@tiipee.com. We respond within 30 days maximum.

You can also file a complaint with the French data protection authority, the CNIL: www.cnil.fr

9. Cookies and trackers

The Tiipee mobile app does not use cookies.

Local technical identifiers (authentication tokens) are stored on your device only to maintain your session. This data is not shared with third parties and is deleted upon logout.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy. In case of a substantial change, you will be informed by email or in-app notification at least 30 days before the changes take effect.

The current version is always available at: tiipee.com/confidentialite
