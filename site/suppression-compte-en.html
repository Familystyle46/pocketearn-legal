Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your Tiipee account (published by Ludovic PEYRE) and associated data.

How to request deletion

1. By email (parent or child account)
Send a request to contact@tiipee.com with the subject "Account deletion", from the parent account's email address. We process requests within 30 days maximum.
2. Deleting a child profile from the app
The parent can request deletion of a child's profile and data at any time by contacting us at the address above.

Data deleted

Following your request, we permanently delete, within 30 days:

  • The parent account (email address, first name, identifier)
  • Child profiles (first name, identifier)
  • The history of "phone put down" sessions and earnings
  • Screen time data
  • Pocket-money rule configuration
  • The history of payout requests and validations
  • Device identifiers used for notifications

Data retained (legal obligations)

DataRetention periodReason
Billing data10 yearsLegal accounting obligation
Anonymized technical logs90 daysSecurity / debugging

No other data is retained after deletion.

Contact

For any question: contact@tiipee.com. See also our privacy policy.
